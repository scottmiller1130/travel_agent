Getting Started

Works without an account. Sign up to save your trips permanently, unlock higher limits, and collaborate with others.

  1. Open the app in your browser. Your session is created automatically — your trip data is saved so you can pick up where you left off.

  2. Sign in (optional but recommended) — click Sign in in the sidebar to create a free account with email or Google. Your trips, preferences, and itineraries are then tied to your account, not just your browser.

  3. Type your destination in the chat box at the bottom. Be as vague or specific as you like — "I want to go somewhere warm in January" works just as well as "Flights from JFK to Bali on March 15".

  4. The agent will ask follow-up questions if it needs more info — number of travelers, budget, departure city, dates, etc.

  5. Watch the Trip Board (🗺 button, top right) fill in as the agent builds your itinerary.

  6. Keep chatting to refine, change dates, swap hotels, or add activities.

💡
First time? Try one of the quick-start hints below the chat box. Clicking any hint fills it into the message field for you.

Quick-start examples you can click right in the app:

Plan a trip to Bali Flights NYC → Tokyo in July Best month to visit Lisbon? Remember I prefer window seats
👤 Your Account

Creating an Account

A free account ties your trips, preferences, and workspaces to you — not just your browser. Sign in on any device and pick up where you left off.

🔑 How to sign in

  1. Click Sign in in the bottom-left sidebar panel.

  2. Choose Continue with Google for one-click sign-in, or enter your email to receive a magic link / set a password.

  3. You're in. Your avatar and plan badge appear in the sidebar.

ℹ️
The app works fully without an account — anonymous sessions still get flights, hotels, itineraries, and deal hunting. Signing in adds trip history sync, workspaces, and higher usage limits.

👤 Your profile panel

Once signed in, the sidebar shows:

  • Your name and email from your account
  • Plan badge — Free / Pro / Team
  • Usage bar — how many chat turns you've used this month vs. your plan limit
  • Upgrade button — if you're on the Free plan and want more

🔒 Your data is private

All trips, preferences, and itineraries are scoped to your user ID. Other users can never see your data. The only way to share an itinerary is to explicitly generate a share link (see Export & Share).

✈ Flights

Searching Flights

Flight results come from Google Flights via SerpAPI — real prices, real airlines, real departure times. Carbon emission data is included where available.

🔍 What you can search for

🛫

One-way & round-trip

Just mention whether you need a return flight. The agent figures out the rest.

💺

Cabin class

Economy, premium economy, business, or first class — just say which you want.

👨‍👩‍👧

Multiple passengers

Tell the agent how many people are traveling and it prices for all of them.

💰

Price cap

"Under $500" or "budget flights only" — the agent filters results to your limit.

🏙

City or airport code

Say "New York" or "JFK" — both work. The agent resolves city names to the right airport.

🔄

Flexible dates

Ask for the cheapest flights in a date range — the agent compares multiple days.

ℹ️
Flight results show airline, flight number, departure/arrival times, duration, number of stops, and price per person. Up to 5 results are shown by default — ask for more if you need them.

✈ Booking a flight

When the agent finds a flight you like, say "Book it" or "Book the Delta flight". A confirmation modal will pop up showing all the details. You must click Confirm Booking before any action is taken — nothing is booked without your explicit approval.

🏨 Hotels

Finding Hotels

Hotels are searched by city, dates, star rating, and budget. The agent automatically adds hotels to your itinerary.

Example requestWhat it does
"Find a 4-star hotel in Lisbon under $150/night" Searches hotels, filters by rating and price, shows top 3 options
"I need somewhere with a pool and free breakfast" Filters amenities in the search, prioritises matching properties
"Book the Bairro Alto Hotel" Triggers a booking confirmation modal before proceeding
"Find me a cheaper option for night 3" Searches alternatives for a specific night and updates the board
💡
Tip: Set your hotel star preference once ("Remember I prefer 4-star hotels") and the agent will always default to it for future searches.
🎯 Deal Hunting

Finding the Best Prices

Two powerful tools let you find the cheapest time to fly — by date or by month — with a visual price calendar.

📅 Cheapest date finder

Ask: "Find the cheapest dates to fly from New York to Tokyo around March 15"

The agent searches ±7 days around your target date and shows a colour-coded price calendar:

  • 🟢 Green = cheapest dates
  • 🟡 Yellow = mid-range
  • 🔴 Red = most expensive

You can click any date in the calendar to instantly search flights for that day.

📊 Best month finder

Ask: "When is the cheapest month to fly from London to Bali?"

The agent analyses 12 months of pricing and shows a bar chart with:

  • Average price per person each month
  • Season rating (Peak 🔴 / Shoulder 🟡 / Off-peak 🟢)
  • The single best-value month highlighted with a ★ Best badge
  • How much you save vs. the most expensive month

Click any month bar to search flights for that period.

💡
The deal hunter also tells you the cheapest day of the week to fly (e.g. "Tuesdays average $180 less than Fridays on this route").

🖱️ Drag and drop

Cards on the board are fully draggable — on desktop and mobile. Drag a hotel, activity, or flight card to a different day to reorder your trip. Changes are saved automatically.

📄 What each card shows

  • Colour-coded left border: ✈ blue=flight, 🏨 teal=hotel, 🗺 orange=activity, 🍽 gold=restaurant, 🚗 grey=transfer
  • Time — departure or activity start time
  • Price — individual item cost in your chosen currency
  • Status badge — Confirmed / Suggested / Alternative
  • Notes — any details the agent added (e.g. "Window seat requested")

🔒 Locking cards

Hover over any card to see action buttons. Click 🔒 Lock to lock a card in place — it won't move when you drag other items. Click again to unlock.

⚡ Conflict detection

The board automatically detects scheduling issues and shows them in a banner:

  • 🔴 Overlap — two events at the same time
  • 🟡 Tight gap — less than 20 minutes between events
  • 🔵 Free time — a 3+ hour gap with nothing planned (click "Ask agent →" to fill it)

📄 Week-by-week navigation

For trips longer than 7 days, the board shows one week at a time with Prev / Next buttons to keep it fast and readable.

↔ Alternatives

Hover any card and click ↔ Alternative to ask the agent to suggest 2–3 swaps for that specific item.

💰 Budget & Travelers

Budget Tracking & Per-Person Breakdown

Tell the agent your total budget and number of travelers. The board shows the total cost, per-person cost, and a breakdown by category — all in one place.

✈ $3,280 · $820/pp 🏨 $1,120 · $280/pp 🗺 $480 · $120/pp 🍽 $360 · $90/pp

👥 Travelers selector

Use the − / + stepper in the budget panel (bottom of the Trip Board) to adjust the number of travelers at any time. All per-person amounts update instantly — no need to re-ask the agent.

🔢 How budget tracking works

  1. Tell the agent your budget: "Plan a trip to Bali for 4 people, total budget $6,000"

  2. As it searches flights and hotels, it tracks the running total in the board's budget bar.

  3. If a result would bust the budget, the agent warns you and suggests cheaper alternatives.

  4. The bar turns amber at 70% and red at 90% of budget.

  5. Category chips show both total and per-person amounts for flights, hotels, activities, and food.

💡
Tip: All budgets in the agent are total for the whole group. When you say "budget $4,000 for 2 people", the agent targets $4,000 total — $2,000 per person — and shows both in the budget panel.

👥 Workspaces

Collaborative Workspaces

Plan a group trip together. Create a workspace, invite your travel companions, and everyone edits the same itinerary in real time. Available on Pro and Team plans.

🏗 Creating a workspace

  1. Open the Workspaces panel in the sidebar (appears after signing in).

  2. Click + New Workspace and give it a name (e.g. "Italy Trip — Summer 2025").

  3. Optionally link it to your current planning session so invited members can see the itinerary right away.

  4. Click Create. You're the owner.

✉️ Inviting members

  1. Open the workspace and click Invite member.

  2. Enter your travel companion's email address and choose their role:

    RoleCan viewCan chat & editCan manage members
    Editor✓✓—
    Viewer✓——

  3. Click Send invite. Your companion receives an email with a link to join.

ℹ️
Invited members who don't yet have an account will be prompted to sign up when they follow the invite link. Their role is activated automatically once they join.

✈ How workspace planning works

Everyone in the workspace shares the same chat session and Trip Board. When one member updates the itinerary, others see it reflected the next time they load the page. The AI agent responds to whoever sends the most recent message — great for back-and-forth group planning.

🔑 Owner controls

  • Change a member's role at any time from Editor ↔ Viewer
  • Remove a member by clicking the ✕ next to their name
  • Delete the workspace entirely (removes access for all members)
💡
Tip: Use the Viewer role for stakeholders who should see the trip plan but shouldn't be able to change it — like a manager approving a team offsite itinerary.
📤 Export & Share

Exporting & Sharing Your Itinerary

Once your itinerary is built, click the ⬇ Export button (top-right, next to the Trip Board toggle).

📄

Download JSON

Full machine-readable itinerary. You can import this back in at any time to restore or share a trip.

📝

Download Markdown

Clean text version of your itinerary — paste into Notion, Obsidian, or any notes app.

🖨

Print / Save PDF

Opens a clean, print-friendly view. Use your browser's print dialog to save as PDF.

🔗

Share link

Generates a read-only share URL and copies it to your clipboard. Send to travel companions!

📂

Import JSON

Load a previously exported itinerary — yours or someone else's — to continue editing.

🔗
Share links are read-only — recipients can view your itinerary but can't edit it. Share links never expire unless you reset your session.

💡 Pro Tips

Tips & Tricks

Get the most out of your travel agent with these power-user moves.

1️⃣ Start broad, then refine

✅
Start with "Plan a week in Portugal in June" and let the agent build a complete itinerary. Then say "Move Day 3 to the Algarve instead of Porto" or "Replace the hotel with something in the old town" to tweak.

2️⃣ Use the deal hunter first

📅
Before committing to dates, ask "What's the cheapest month to fly to Tokyo?" — you might find flying two weeks earlier saves $400 per person.

3️⃣ Lock flights and hotels, flex activities

🔒
Once you're happy with a flight or hotel, lock the card on the board. Then ask the agent to "Suggest more activities for Day 4" without risk of your booked items being moved.

4️⃣ Import itineraries from friends

📂
If a friend shares their trip JSON with you, use Export → Import JSON to load it. Then ask the agent to "Adapt this itinerary for a solo traveler" or "What would this cost in May instead of August?"

5️⃣ Ask for alternatives by the card

↔
Hover any board card and click ↔ Alternative to get 2–3 options for just that one item — without rebuilding your whole trip.

6️⃣ Use your travel history

📚
The agent remembers everything in your current conversation. Ask "Based on the Lisbon trip we just planned, how does it compare to doing Greece instead?" for instant side-by-side analysis.

7️⃣ Reset vs. New Session

ℹ️
↺ Reset conversation (bottom of the sidebar) clears the chat history but keeps your saved preferences and trips intact. Use it to start planning a new trip without losing your settings.
